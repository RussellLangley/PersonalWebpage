The City of Peterborough


Figure 1 The Liftlocks, one of Peterborough's proud momuments. Source

City Information

  • Province: Ontario
  • Country: Canada
  • Population: 147,890
  • Mayor: Diane Therrien

Peterborough was once known as a flourishing city with a growing industy presence. Unfortunately, this is no longer the case and many of these keystone businesses have since relocated or closed down. At present, Peterborough is known as a retirement city, housing a staggering amount of retirement homes. Arguably the only thing of note now in Peterborough is the once famous Liftlocks, which championed the highest hydraulic boat lift in the world for some time. It was able to raise boats upwards of 65 ft.
